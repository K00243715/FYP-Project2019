About

This website aims to inform users of accessible tools that you can adapt for your webpage.

Accessibility features enhances the user experience for all types of users especially for those suffering from dyslexia.

Who is this website for?

This website is for educators, parents and children who have dyslexia or know somebody who has dyslexia.
